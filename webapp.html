Web and Mobile App Development

I will put my experiences with web and mobile app development on this page, as I have done a bit of both.

Mobile App Development

I taught myself to make mobile apps. I started with Xcode and Swift, but as I gained more knowledge I also learned to make Android apps with Android Studio and Java as well as cross-platform apps that could be developed for both iOS and Android at the same time with Flutter and Dart.

Eventually, I also got involved with a mobile app development group on campus. This group was put together by the Mizzou Computing Association (MCA). I joined in the winter of 2020, right before the pandemic hit, and once that happened the group fizzled out before we got to build anything together.

After this, I became a TA for two iOS app development courses through Mizzou's IT department. More information for this can be found on my home page under the teaching tab.

All of this resulted in many apps. There were two that ended up on Apple's app store:
  • Multiplication Tables
  • RGB Colors
Note that they are no longer up because I cancelled my Apple Developer membership.
